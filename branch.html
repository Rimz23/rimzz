Branch Details
Downtown Oasis, New York

As you step into Downtown Oasis, you'll immediately sense the vibrant energy of the city. Yet, within our doors, you'll discover a haven of modern accommodations designed for your comfort. Immerse yourself in the urban charm of New York while enjoying the perfect balance of city life and relaxation
Location: Situated in the heart of the bustling city center, our Downtown Oasis branch offers convenience and urban charm

Coastal Retreat, Malibu

Our Coastal Retreat is a haven of serenity where you can unwind amidst the natural beauty of Malibu. Whether you're a beach enthusiast, a surfing aficionado, or simply seeking a tranquil escape, this idyllic getaway invites you to bask in the warm California sun and embrace the coastal lifestyle
Location: Nestled along the stunning coastline of Malibu, California, our Coastal Retreat provides breathtaking ocean views

Alpine Lodge, Swiss Alps

The Alpine Lodge beckons you to embrace the pristine beauty of nature. Whether you're an avid hiker, a skiing enthusiast, or simply seeking solace in the tranquil alpine landscape, our lodge provides the perfect backdrop for your mountain adventure. Enjoy the best of outdoor activities, paired with cozy accommodations, in this alpine paradise
Location: Located in the picturesque Swiss Alps, the Alpine Lodge offers a tranquil escape amidst stunning mountain scenery
